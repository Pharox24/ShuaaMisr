SHUAA MISR

Shuaa Misr For Investment & Development S.A.E

Shuaa Misr International Company for Investment and Development is an Egyptian joint stock company with a capital of 10 million Egyptian pounds. As a multi-activity enterprise, the company was founded on a wealth of expertise across various industries, including but not limited to:

Our Services

  • International Trade – Trading various commodities such as petroleum products, oil well drilling chemicals, and cement.
  • Logistics Services – Providing international logistics solutions, including sea, land, and air freight, as well as customs clearance.
  • Agricultural Processing – Sorting, drying, and packaging a wide range of Egyptian agricultural crops.
  • Consumer Goods – Distribution and sale of natural bottled water.
  • Medical Supplies – Supplying high-quality medical equipment and products.
  • Advertising Services – Offering innovative marketing and advertising solutions.
  • Tourism & Adventure – Organizing desert safari experiences.

Global Reach

With branches in Kuwait and the United States under the trade name EGCSS, we have built a global presence, serving a wide network of esteemed clients and trusted agents. Our extensive reach enables us to deliver top-tier services to clients anywhere in the world.
