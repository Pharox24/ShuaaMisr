Shuaa Misr International Company for Investment and Development (SMIFID) is a leading Egyptian joint stock company with an authorized capital of 10 million Egyptian pounds. Established as a natural evolution of extensive expertise across multiple industries, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to global market needs.

Our Key Sectors

  • International Trade: Petroleum products, oil well drilling chemicals, and cement.
  • Logistics: Global freight and customs clearance.
  • Agricultural Processing: Sorting, drying, and packaging of Egyptian crops.
  • Beverage Distribution: Natural bottled water supply.
  • Medical Supplies: High-quality medical equipment.
  • Tourism & Adventure: Desert safari experiences.

Our Experience

With a proven track record and deep industry expertise, SMIFID delivers excellence across its diverse operations, ensuring high standards and efficiency in every sector we serve.

Our global presence extends through strategically located branches in Kuwait and the United States under the trade name EGCSS. Backed by a vast network of trusted clients and reliable partners, we deliver exceptional services worldwide, making us the preferred partner for businesses seeking excellence in international trade, logistics, and beyond.
