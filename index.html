Shuaa Misr For Investment & Development S.A.E

Welcome to Shuaa Misr International for Investment & Development S.A.E., a leading Egyptian company with a strong presence in international trading and logistics. With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in global markets, providing high-quality services and solutions tailored to the needs of our clients. Our branches in Kuwait and the USA enable us to expand our reach and operate seamlessly across different regions. Our diverse business portfolio covers a wide range of industries, including oil drilling contracting, oil field chemicals, fertilizers, logistics, and international trading. We specialize in the supply of diesel and petrol, ensuring reliable energy solutions, and we take pride in exporting premium-quality Egyptian fruits to international markets. With a commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we strive to contribute to economic growth and strengthen trade connections worldwide.
